About Us

Matthew’s Tree Care

Matthew’s Tree Care is a locally owned and operated tree service based in Tallahassee, Florida. We’re committed to doing things the right way — with safety, skill, and integrity at the core of every job.

With certified arborists on staff and years of hands-on experience, we provide expert tree removal, pruning, stump grinding, and emergency storm response. Our team combines professional-grade work with a friendly, small-town attitude you can count on.

Whether you're facing storm damage or planning routine care, we bring the tools, knowledge, and respect your property deserves. We proudly serve homeowners, businesses, and property managers throughout Tallahassee and surrounding areas.
